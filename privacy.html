Lemniscate Education

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Lemniscate Education is a California-based 501(c)(3) non-profit organization ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or participate in our programs.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly. When you interact with us or our sponsored projects, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Any additional information you choose to provide
  • Payment information (processed by third-party providers; we do not store payment card details)

Information collected automatically. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses in server logs as part of operating the hosting service. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for more details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and manage our programs and sponsored projects
  • Communicate with participants about events, logistics, and updates
  • Process fees and manage registrations

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our programs, only as necessary.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to operate our programs and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us.

Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at directors@lemniscate.education.

Children's Privacy

Our programs are designed for students. We collect student information with the understanding that parents or guardians are involved in the registration process and have consented to their student's participation. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at directors@lemniscate.education.
